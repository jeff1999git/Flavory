# **App Name**: Flavory

## Core Features:

- Homepage Showcase: Display a homepage showcasing featured food products with appealing visuals and concise descriptions.
- Dynamic Product Listing: List food products with title, image, price, brief description, and category tags.
- Product Search and Filtering: Allow users to search for products by name or keywords, and filter products by category.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Saturated yellowish-brown (#B8860B) for an organic and appetizing feel, suggesting natural ingredients.
- Background color: Light beige (#F5F5DC), a desaturated version of the primary color to provide a subtle, warm backdrop.
- Accent color: Earthy green (#6B8E23), analogous to the primary color to evoke feelings of freshness and health.
- Body text: 'PT Sans' for body text -- a warm and modern sans-serif font providing a readable and friendly feel. Headline text: 'Playfair' for headlines, giving a contrasting stylish touch.
- Use simple, line-based icons to represent product categories and filter options.
- Employ a clean, card-based layout for product listings to ensure a mobile-friendly and visually organized experience.
- Implement subtle fade-in animations on image loading and product card appearance to enhance perceived performance.
